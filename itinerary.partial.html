Itinerary

  • Day 1: Yellowstone National Park

    Be wowed by bubbling mud pots, spouting geysers and steaming vents in Yellowstone. Hike through the very heart of the landmark in Upper Geyser Basin, noticing all the sights, sounds—and smells!—of this miraculous wonderland. Show your kids the fumaroles of Biscuit Basin and legendary Old Faithful geyser up close.

  • Day 2:Keep Exploring

    Behold the Grand Canyon the Yellowstone, where waterfalls cascade over pastel-colored cliffs. Continue the walk through alpine meadows and stands of lodgepole pine along the canyon rim. Enjoy every minute of touching the wild nature.

  • Day 3-4:Grand Tetons

    Start the morning with hiking, get your first glimpse of the majestic Grand Tetons. We start exploring Grand Teton National Park, an incredible spot of wildflower-strewn meadows, shimmering lakes, thick woodland and sawtooth mountains. Our group walks through conifer forests and aspen groves near the shore of Jackson Lake. There we take a boat and explore Cascade Canyon. The local kids can teach the kids rock climbing. Camping with fantastic views of the Teton peaks.

  • Day 5:Teton Range

    This is our last day of adventures. We pedal beneath the Teton Range, pass weathered barns and grazing horses. Get ready to meet a roaming bison! | Our guides will tell everything about wilderness preservation—especially inspiring as you take in this amazing landscape.
