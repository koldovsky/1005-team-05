Itinerary

  • Day 1: Yellowstone National Park

    Arrive at the park and check into your accommodation. Spend the day exploring the park's geothermal features, including the famous Old Faithful geyser. Take a hike to see the Grand Prismatic Spring, the largest hot spring in the park.

  • Day 2: Lamar Valley and Wildlife Watching

    Head to Lamar Valley for the best chance to see wildlife such as bison, elk, wolves, and bears. Bring your binoculars and a camera to capture these incredible animals in their natural habitat. After a day of wildlife watching, return to your accommodation for a restful evening.

  • Day 3-4: Grand Teton National Park

    Drive to Grand Teton National Park, where you'll spend the next two days. Take a scenic drive along the park's main road, stopping at lookout points to take in the stunning views of the Teton Range. Go for a hike in the mountains, or take a boat tour of Jenny Lake. Don't forget to take some time to relax and enjoy the peaceful surroundings.

  • Day 5: Teton Range

    Spend your last day in the Teton Range taking in the beauty of the mountains. Go for a hike, take a bike ride, or simply relax and enjoy the scenery. In the evening, return to your accommodation to rest and prepare for your departure the next day.
